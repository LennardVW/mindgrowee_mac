MindGrowee is a beautiful, native macOS app for tracking habits and journaling your thoughts. Built with SwiftUI and designed for productivity.

KEY FEATURES:

• Habit Tracking
Create daily habits and track your progress. Each day is a fresh start - build streaks and watch your consistency grow.

• Journal with Mood Tracking
Record your thoughts, track your mood (1-5 stars), and organize with tags. Look back on your journey anytime.

• Statistics & Insights
View detailed statistics about your habits, streaks, and mood trends. Identify patterns and celebrate progress.

• Menu Bar Access
Quick access from your menu bar. Check off habits without opening the main window.

• Focus Modes
Create custom focus modes for different contexts - Morning Routine, Work, Weekend, etc.

• Categories
Organize habits into categories: Health, Fitness, Productivity, Learning, and more.

• Notifications
Set reminders for habits and get evening summaries of your progress.

• Privacy First
All data is stored locally on your Mac. No accounts, no cloud, complete privacy.

• Import/Export
Export your data in JSON, Markdown, or CSV format. Import from other apps.

• Widgets
Add widgets to your Notification Center for quick habit tracking.

• Dark Mode
Full support for dark mode with customizable accent colors.

PERFECT FOR:
• Building new habits
• Breaking bad habits  
• Tracking daily routines
• Journaling and reflection
• Mood tracking
• Productivity improvement
